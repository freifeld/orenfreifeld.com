I'm an Associate Professor in the Institute for Applied Artificial Intelligence within the Faculty of Computer and Information Science at Ben-Gurion University.

Earlier, I was a postdoc at MIT CSAIL (Advisor: John Fisher). I earned my PhD in Applied Mathematics from Brown University (Advisor: Michael Black). During my PhD, I was also a visiting student at Stanford University, Electrical Engineering (Host: Krishna Shenoy).

Founded in 2016, VIL is among the very few groups in Israel that publish consistently in top-tier conferences in both CV (CVPR/ICCV/ECCV) and ML (NeurIPS/ICML/AISTATS/UAI).
