Research Summary

My group works in the fields of computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning. We usually focus in unsupervised or self-supervised settings, with applications involving images, videos, or time series. Our recent and current research focuses on:

  1. Deep learning models for geometric alignment (for time series, 2D images, or 3D scenes). Example papers: Hauberg et al., AISTATS 2016
    Shapira-Weber et al., NeurIPS 2019
    Shapira-Weber & Freifeld, ICML 2023
    Barel et al., ECCV 2024
    Shapira Weber et al., ICML 2025
    Naaman et al., ICCV 2025
    Shapira Weber & Freifeld, arXiv 2025
    Hirsch et al., NeurIPS 2025

    (many of these works above are built upon our efficient and highly expressive CPAB diffeomorphisms [Freifeld et al., ICCV 2015], [Freifeld et al., PAMI 2017] and/or our diffeomorphic spatial transformer networks [Skafte et al., CVPR 2018])

  2. Video analysis. Example papers: Chelly et al., CVPR 2020
    Erez et al., ECCV 2022
    Uziel et al., NeurIPS 2023
    Naaman et al., ICCV 2025

  3. Generic, task-agnostic improvements in deep learning, such as wavelet convolutions for large receptive fields and highly-expressive trainable activation functions. Example papers: Chelly et al., ECCV 2024
    Finder et al., ECCV 2024
    Finder et al., ICML 2025

  4. 3D reconstruction and novel view synthesis using Gaussian Splatting (3DGS), typically enhanced by geometric deep features. Example papers: Mualem et al., arXiv 2024
    Amoyal et al., CVPR 2026

  5. Bayesian nonparametric (BNP) clustering, particularly fast, scalable inference for mixture models and deep models for BNP clustering. Example papers: Uziel et al., ICCV 2019
    Dinari et al., HPML 2019
    Dinari & Freifeld, UAI 2020
    Dinari et al., arXiv 2022
    Dinari & Freifeld, AISTATS 2022
    Dinari & Freifeld, UAI 2022
    Winter et al., AISTATS 2022
    Ronen et al., CVPR 2022
    Uziel et al., ICML 2025
    Chelly et al., AISTATS 2025

  6. Underwater computer vision. Example papers: Mualem et al., arXiv 2024

My earlier work focused on statistics on manifolds (Lie groups and/or Riemannian manifolds) and modeling shape deformations. Example papers: Freifeld et al., CVPR 2010
